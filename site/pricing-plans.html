Common questions.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — we offer a guided trial so you can explore Collage with your own content. Talk to our team to get started.

How does pricing work?

Pricing is based on your team size and storage needs. Every plan includes the full platform — no feature gating. Schedule a demo and we'll put together a quote that fits your team.

Are there long-term contracts?

We offer both monthly and annual plans. Annual plans include a discount. No multi-year commitments required.

What does onboarding look like?

We handle migration, setup, and training. Most teams are fully onboarded within a week — not months.

Can I migrate from another DAM?

Absolutely. We've migrated teams from Brandfolder, Bynder, Canto, and others. We handle the heavy lifting so nothing gets lost.

What kind of support is included?

Every plan includes direct access to our team via email and chat. No tiered support — you always talk to a real person who knows the product.
