Customer Story

How E4 arms hundreds of advisors with a self-serve content portal

By Ross Durbin · March 10, 2026

A two-person marketing team replaced Brandfolder and SharePoint folder chaos with a color-coded, collage-based library that field advisors and partners navigate on their own.

Company E4
Industry Financial Services
Team Size 2 primary users
Use Case Sales Enablement & Distribution
The Customer

A lean marketing team powering an advisor network

E4 is a financial services firm managing insurance solutions across four lines: Life, Disability, Annuities, and more. Jessie Carlquist and one colleague in marketing are responsible for creating, organizing, and distributing all sales tools, educational materials, and campaign content to a network of field advisors and Regional Vice Presidents.

Their audience ranges from tech-savvy RVPs to older advisors who need the simplest possible experience to find and use the right content.

The Challenge

An inherited system that nobody could leverage

Before Collage, E4 had inherited a Brandfolder account, but without adequate support or training the team couldn't get real value from it. Day-to-day content still lived in SharePoint — deeply nested folder structures that even internal team members struggled to navigate. External advisors had an even harder time. Every content request flowed through marketing, turning Jessie's team into a bottleneck.

The team needed a platform simple enough that hundreds of advisors could browse, find, and download the right materials without training or support — while giving marketing the tools to keep everything organized and up to date.

1

Color-coded collages that advisors navigate intuitively

E4 organizes content into collages by insurance line — Life, Disability, Annuities — each with its own distinct color. Red for disability, for example. Advisors open the brand portal, see the visual grid, and know exactly where to go.

Assets live in multiple collages when relevant. A webinar about life insurance appears in both the Educational Library and the Life Insurance collage, so advisors always find it no matter how they browse.

"They intuitively figure out where to look. Everything is alphabetical, color-coded — it's just easy for them to navigate."
2

Version history keeps weekly reports on a single, permanent link

Every Monday, Jessie updates the Annuity Solutions Weekly (ASW) — a PDF report with current annuity statistics. Instead of uploading a new file each week, she uses version history to replace the content in place. The share link in the weekly email never changes.

This simple workflow eliminates broken links, keeps the library clean, and means advisors always see the latest data when they click through from any past email.

"I just drag and drop, it updates the file name automatically, and the link in the email always works. It's really simple."
3

Share links that work for everyone — no login required

E4's advisors and RVPs aren't employees — they're external partners. Jessie uses share links by default for every piece of content she distributes, ensuring anyone with the link can access it without authentication.

4

Advisors self-serve instead of emailing marketing

Before Collage, every content request went through Jessie's team. Now advisors browse the brand portal on their own — checking for existing materials before ever reaching out. The visual grid with large thumbnails makes it easy to scan, even for less tech-savvy users.

The result: fewer inbound requests, less duplicate work, and advisors who feel empowered to find what they need.

"It's easier for them to just go to Collage than to navigate SharePoint. They can check for themselves if something already exists before asking us."
"We're really happy with how it's going so far. We like it a lot. It's simple, it's intuitive — advisors can find what they need without us having to walk them through it."
Jessie Carlquist, Marketing Communications

The impact

Moving to Collage transformed how E4 distributes content to their advisor network.

Advisor self-service

Hundreds of field advisors browse and download content without emailing marketing

Evergreen links

Weekly reports update in place via version history — one link works forever

Visual organization

Color-coded collages by insurance line make navigation instant and intuitive

Zero-friction sharing

Share links give external partners instant access — no login walls
